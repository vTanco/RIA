100% Non-Profit Initiative

Research Integrity Analyzer is a free, open-source tool designed to promote transparency in scientific research. We don't charge for our services, but maintaining and improving this platform involves ongoing costs including server infrastructure, AI processing, and continuous development.

Your support helps us keep this tool free and accessible to researchers, students, and institutions worldwide. Every contribution, no matter how small, makes a difference in our mission to combat conflicts of interest in scientific literature.
